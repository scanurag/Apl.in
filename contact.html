APL is a premier cricket tournament endorsed by the Arunachal Cricket Association (ARCA) under the affiliation of the BCCI. The Arunachal Premier League is designed to discover and promote the immense cricketing talent in Arunachal Pradesh and beyond. With a focus on nurturing young, passionate cricketers, APL provides a platform for players to showcase their skills on a national stage. The commitment and vision of the APL organizers have elevated the league to both national and international recognition. APL ensures that no passionate cricketer goes unnoticed, offering opportunities for players aged 9 and above to shine and potentially secure spots in BCCI Domestic Season tournaments.
